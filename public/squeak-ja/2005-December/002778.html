アドレスは私に届きますので，ご質問は日本語で送って頂いて結構です。

ます。また，推奨ホテル(Doubletree Berkeley Marina)への宿泊予約も，

http://www.db.soc.i.kyoto-u.ac.jp/c5-06/local.html#hotel

より御利用頂けます。12月31日(延長されました)までに予約しますと，会議割引
が適用されます。バンケットもこのホテルで行われる予定になっていますので，
ぜひ御利用下さい。

皆様多数の御参加をお待ちしております。

Call for Demos and Posters

The Fourth International Conference on Creating, Connecting and
Collaborating through Computing (C5 2006) will have a demo and poster
session, in order to encourage a more interactive exchange of ideas
and reports on work that is complete as well as work in progress.

We have allocated 90 minutes for the demo and poster session, with an
additional 30 minutes available before the session for preparation and
set-up. People who would like to exhibit their work at the demo and
poster session should submit the following information to
c5-06 ＠ db.soc.i.kyoto-u.ac.jp by January 10, 2006.

Title:
Exhibitor's name:
Abstract:
Necessary Equipment: Desk, Poster board, power outlet, wireless network
                     (please indicate items you need)

The conference will provide a desk and/or a poster board, a power
outlet (AC 120V with US-spec plug), and wireless network
connection. Please bring other equipment (such as a laptop PC)
necessary for your demo.

If you have any question, please feel free to contact us at 
c5-06 ＠ db.soc.i.kyoto-u.ac.jp.

We are looking forward to meeting you in Berkeley, California.

Best Regards,

C5 2006 Program Co-Chairs

Rieko Kadobayashi
