阿部@新百合です。

Alan Kayさんが主宰するNPOであるViewpoints Resarch InstituteのIan
Piumartaさんが来日し、現在開発中の新しい言語について何件かの講演を行いま
す。
この言語は、しばしば「Ianのやつ」として言及されているもので、Coke&Pepsi
やGolden Boxなどと呼ばれることもあります。
今回の講演は未来のSqueakというかDynabookが進む方向を占う上で、重要なもの
になると思われます。

第10回プログラミングおよびプログラミング言語ワークショップ
PPL2008
http://www.nue.riec.tohoku.ac.jp/ppl2008/
主催: 日本ソフトウェア科学会 プログラミング論研究会  
日程: 2008年3月5日(水)〜3月7日(金)  
会場: 仙台市 秋保温泉 緑水亭  

招待講演 Ian Piumarta 氏 (Viewpoints Research Institute) 
タイトル Steps Toward the Reinvention of Programming 
要旨 Fundamental research can be practical, concrete and create
immediately useful artefacts. Powerful examples of fundamental research
that were practical and had immediate benefits were the inventions of
the networked personal computer, dynamic object-oriented programming,
the graphical user interface, and the Internet. An excruciating example
of an area that still needs much more than incremental improvement is
programming, both in the large and in the small. Code is too: large,
complex, costly, buggy, insecure, segregated, and inexpressive. We have
plans to attempt a qualitative reinvention of programming and to start
one of the subprojects this year: to make a practical working
mathematical model of a complete personal computer system. 
In his talk Dr. Piumarta will give an overview of this five-year
research project at Viewpoints Research Institute, funded by the U.S.
National Science Foundation. He will discuss the background and
philosophy of the approach, and will demonstrate parts of the underlying
architecture created so far.
