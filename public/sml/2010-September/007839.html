Dear Smalltalkers,

this year ESUG would like to reward effort made in book publications.
Since we want to push new books but that it takes time to write books
we will take into account a window of  5 years.
So you are requested to vote for the best book of the last 5 years.

http://www.surveymonkey.com/s/Q2LKKDT

of these nice free books :
- Squeak by Example: http://www.squeakbyexample.org/
- Pharo by Example: http://www.pharobyexample.org/

If think this is really important to have Smalltalk documentations and
book in japanese if you want to attract more people in the community
;-)
If you want to help translating these two books, please ask me in private.
